Interactive Bubble Chart

The interactive bubble chart shows the relationship between population size and energy consumption across Australian states. The size of the bubbles indicates total energy consumption for each state, while the x-axis represents population. We can see that the population size greatly influences the total energy consumption for that State.As we can see that both New South Wales and Victoria having the highest populations both have the highest energy consumption as opose to Tasimina having the lowest population and the lowest energy consumption.
